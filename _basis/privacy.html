  4. LOG-FILES 
    At every access to a MS-Control website, usage data are transmitted through the respective Internet browser and stored in log files, the so-called server log files. The records stored in this case contain the following data: date and time of the retrieval, name of the accessed page, IP address, referrer URL (the previously visited page), the amount of data transferred, and browser type / version. We are evaluating these log file records to further enhance and make our offerings more user-friendly, to find and fix bugs faster, and to control server capacity. At the same time we use the log file records for system security reasons to defend against attacks, e.g. in the context of so-called distributed-denial-of-service attacks, which aim to block access to our pages by overloading requests. We save log files for 1 month.

  13. Your Rights

    Right to information
    You can request information about whether and which data we process to what extent.

    Right to rectification
    If your data is incomplete or incorrect, you can request a correction and / or completion at any time.

    Right to delete
    You may contact us with a deletion request if we improperly process it, if the processing disproportionately interferes with your legitimate interests, if the data for the purposes for which it was collected are no longer necessary, if you have revoked your consent and the processing of the data can not be based on any other legal basis or that deletion is necessary to fulfill a legal obligation. Please note that there may be reasons for an immediate cancellation, e.g. statutory storage requirements.

    Right to restriction of processing
    You may request a restriction on the processing of your data if
    - You deny the accuracy of your data for a period that allows us to verify the accuracy
    - the processing is illegal and you reject the deletion of your data and instead want a
       restriction
    - We no longer need the data for processing purposes, but you need it for the purposes
       of asserting, exercising or defending legal claims, or
    - You have lodged an objection to the data processing

    Right to data portability
    You may request that you receive the data you provide us in a structured, common and machine-readable format, provided that the processing is done on the basis of your consent or a contract, and the processing is done by automated means.

    Right to objection
    You have the right to object at any time to the processing of personal data concerning you, which is necessary for the protection of our legitimate interests or that of a third party, for reasons arising from your particular situation. Your data will no longer be processed unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds for processing that outweigh your interests, rights and freedoms, or processing for the purposes of asserting, exercising and defending legal claims. You can object to the sending of advertisements at any time without stating reasons.

    Asserting the rights
    To assert your rights, please contact: info@msc.io or the contact options described above. To protect your rights and privacy, we may request additional information to verify your identity (such as official photo ID). Appropriate inquiries will be answered free of charge and as soon as possible, observing the legal deadline.

    Right of appeal
    If you believe that the processing of your data violates data protection law, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with the regulatory authority. In Austria the data protection authority is responsible.
